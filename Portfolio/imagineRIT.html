Imagine RIT Poster

ROLE

Visual Design

WHEN

2017

CLIENT

School Project

For the Imagine RIT poster contest, students in RIT's NMDE elements III class were tasked with creating a poster for Imagine RIT, a science and art showcase event throughout RIT meant to display the works of the best minds RIT has to offer. The poster had to reflect the event and it's feel, as well as inspiring and attracting the main audience/viewers of the poster (Parents & Their children) to go to the event.

Here lies the main iterations of my Imagine RIT Poster.

Attempt 01:

At first I decided to push up the illustrious factor of the Imagine RIT event. I wanted to make the composition inspire curiosity as well as looking very futuristic with interesting colors. I also wanted to use this as an oppurtunity to study lighting, and caustics in c4d, so I created a diamond as a focal point in the composition. After completing this iteration of this poster, I found that I needed to take a different Direction, and so I went for a more industrial feel.

Attempt 02:

For my second attempt at the poster, I wanted to iron down the core feeling and nature of Imagine RIT, the joy of Creation.

The Images down below are just alternately textured and lit variations of my poster.

And Finally

The aesthetics of the final design were made after consulting with my roommates, classmates, and teachers. I ultimately decided to simplify and adapt the other parts of the composition not to take to much attention away from the main design in order to illustrate the core focus.
