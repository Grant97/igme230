Bite Sized Content

Odds of survival is a wildife education app. designed to provide animal safety tips under the framing of how likely you are to survival an encounter with them.

ROLE

Concepting, Motion & Visual Design

WHEN

September 5-17, 2018

CLIENT

University of Michigan

01

Problem

I needed to create an animation that was inviting and fun, while evoking the iconic traits of the University of Michigan.

02

Research

In order to create an animation within such a short turnaround date, I needed to research and breakdown other short animations to figure out what made them successful.

Main Takeaways

Layered Animation

The use of layering animations in the form of pre-compositions and mattes allowed motion designers to create varied and complex animations

Utilizing Abstract Subject matter

By utilizing abstract shape animation, motion designers are able to design animations that can entrance and entertain anyone, which needing them to draw upon prior knowledge.

Engaging Visualization

Parts of an application that may have otherwise been mundane are given interactive and interesting visualization via particle effects, three dimensionality, etc, in order to capture user attention.

Subtle Callbacks

When utilizing references and callbacks to previously existing source materials, many animators will put them in subtly in order to not interrupt the flow of the animation. So that in the case that the viewer does not get reference it won't stifle their enjoyment of the overall animation.

03

Solution

After a lot of planning, research, sketching, and a couple of sleepless nights, I was able to create the intial animation.

Animation Highlights

Transitions

I wanted to use this animation as an exercise in creating smooth transitions utilizing shape layers and camera movement in Adobe After Effects.

Variation

I also wanted to vary the pace of the animation from graceful and slow, to kinetic and fast. In order to keep viewers interested within the short time span of the animation.

Fireworks

I decided to use fireworks as the main motif for my animation, as it was a visually entertaining element that everyone can enjoy, that was also really fun to animate. I mean who doesn't love fireworks?

Animating Text

For the ending text I wanted to make sure that it was inviting and fun, so I animated it in a kinetic and bouncy way while still incorporating the core motif of fireworks.

04

Final Animation
